How to Play!

  • Walk around! This moves your character!
  • Press the Space Button to launch your grapple hook!
  • Pop as many Bubbles as you can before they hit you!
  • Each time a demoralizing bubble hits you, you lose a point of resolve!
  • Play until you run out of resolve!
